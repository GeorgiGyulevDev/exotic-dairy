FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions.

What is Exotic Dairy?

A functional bio-dairy initiative focused on camel and donkey milk products, R&D and future experience-based ecosystems.

Why camel milk?

Camel milk offers a rare nutritional profile and strong potential for functional foods, wellness and specialized nutrition.

Why donkey milk?

Donkey milk is one of the rarest premium dairy sources, suitable for high-value niche products and innovation.

When will products be available?

Selected products are being prepared for launch, while others are in development through research and product testing.

What is the Experience Hub?

A future destination combining animals, education, research, product tasting and public engagement.

How can I participate?

Participation opportunities will be announced as the ecosystem develops.
